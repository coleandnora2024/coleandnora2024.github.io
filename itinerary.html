Itinerary

On this page you'll find everything you need to know about our three day wedding adventure in Italy. Our wedding will officially start on Firday afternoon and end on Sunday after breakfast! Of course we would love for you all to be there for all parts of the wedding, but if, for some reason, you are unable to make it to any of it please let us know as soon as possible! Happy reading of the itinerary!

Day one - arrivals

The activities of the first day are largely straightforward -- arrive, eat, drink, and be merry!

  • 5:00 pm - 7:00 pm

    • Welcome reception on the terrace: After you arrive and check in, you'll be invited to join us for a drinks reception on the terrace.

  • 7:00 pm - 10:30 pm

    • Dinner: Family style dinner served with local Italian wine. Yes, the bar will be open afterwards, however, we have a full day of scheduled fun on Day 2, so don't drink too much!

Day two - Wedding day!!!

Day two is the big day! As you'll see from the itinerary below, there will be breakfast and lunch, however, until the ceremony, the schedule is pretty free. This could be a great time to go explore Fano, go to the beach, or hang out at the pool.

  • 08:00 am - 10:00 am

    • Coffee and croissants: Start your day right with some (or lots of) coffee and croissants overlooking the Villa's gardens.

  • 12:30 pm - 02:00 pm

    • Lunch: Freshly made paninis to take away or to sit down and enjoy. This will be a lite lunch to save room for all the food later (keep reading).

  • 04:30 pm - 06:00 pm

    • Ceremony: No one panic (yet); we're not going to make you sit and watch us be adorable giving speeches for an hour and a half, these timings are yet to be finalised (British spelling -- second compromise of the marriage).

  • 06:00 pm - 08:00 pm

    • Wedding reception: Finger foods and wine to celebrate the happy couple (it's us, we're the happy couple).

  • 08:00 pm - 10:00 pm

    • Dinner: Three course meal in the Villa's garden with local wines and a fresh fruit buffet.

  • 10:00 pm - 10:30 pm

    • Cake: You've made it this far, congrats! For this, you get cake and more wine (this time with bubbles).

  • 10:30 pm - 01:00 am

    • Afterparty: Time to dance! If you're still going strong at this point, don't worry, the bar staff will be available until 01:00 am to buy more beer, wine, or cocktails.

  • All evening long

    • Ice cream cart: In case this wasn't enough food for you, there will be an adorable little ice cream truck available all evening.
