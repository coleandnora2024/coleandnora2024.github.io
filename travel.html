Villa Giulia

Villa Giulia is a historic Monastery nestled within the Marche hills that has now been turned into a lovely Villa surrounded by greenery and overlooking the Adriatic Sea. The Villa has everything that we will need for our wedding weekend (yay!) including a team that will provide all our food using locally sources produce, a well-stocked bar (don't worry, we checked), olive groves and a swimming pool. It's also walking distance to the ocean (for anyone who doesn't like chlorine) and a short drive away from the historic town of Fano.

Accomodation

We would love for you to join us in the Villa which has a range of beautifully decorated rooms and apartments. The accommodation costs 150 EUR per person for the two nights (Friday through Sunday). We have reserved all of the rooms at the Villa so please ....

Food

One of the reasons we picked this venue is because the food is from their organic and local garden. We will be enjoying serveal meals together throughout the weekend on the terrace, in the garden and overlooking the hills and sea.
